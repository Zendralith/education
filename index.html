Courses We Offer

This art and design program offers a dynamic blend of creative disciplines, starting with Traditional & Digital Art, where students develop foundational skills in drawing, painting, and digital illustration. The Graphic & Visual Design course focuses on visual communication through typography, layout, and branding across various media. User Experience & Interface explores human-centered design, teaching students to craft intuitive and engaging digital experiences. Finally, Creative Thinking & Theory sharpens conceptual skills by delving into design history, aesthetics, and innovative problem-solving approaches that fuel meaningful artistic expression.
